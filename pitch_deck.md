## Slide 1: Problem & Who's Affected

### The Mental Health Crisis in India

**The Problem:**
- **1 in 7 Indians** (14% of population) suffer from mental health disorders, yet **85% don't receive treatment**
- Traditional therapy has **critical barriers**: 
  - High costs (â‚¹1,500-5,000/session), unaffordable for most Indians
  - Severe shortage: Only **0.75 psychiatrists per 100,000** population (WHO recommends 3)
  - Deep-rooted **social stigma**: Mental health seen as taboo, "weakness", or spiritual issue
  - **Geographic barriers**: 70% of mental health professionals in urban areas, leaving rural India underserved
- **Cultural barriers**: Family pressure, "log kya kahenge" (what will people say), arranged marriage concerns
- **Accessibility issues**: Limited availability, language barriers, lack of awareness
- **Privacy concerns**: Fear of judgment, family interference, social ostracization

**Who's Affected:**
- **Primary Users**: 
  - **Urban professionals** (22-40 years): IT sector, corporate employees facing work stress, burnout
  - **Students** (16-25 years): Academic pressure, competitive exams (JEE, NEET, UPSC), career anxiety
  - **Women** (18-45 years): Postpartum depression, domestic stress, career-family balance
  - **Rural population**: Limited access, travel costs, lack of awareness
  - **LGBTQ+ community**: High stigma, family rejection, identity struggles
- **Secondary Users**: 
  - **Licensed therapists/counselors**: Expand reach beyond physical clinics
  - **Community organizers**: Create local wellness events, support groups
  - **Educational institutions**: Student mental health programs
  - **Corporate HR**: Employee wellness programs

**Market Size:**
- India's mental health market: **â‚¹5,000+ crores** (growing at 15% CAGR)
- Digital mental health market: **â‚¹1,200+ crores by 2027** (CAGR 25%+)
- **Smartphone penetration**: 750M+ users, 4G/5G connectivity
- **Growing awareness**: Post-COVID mental health conversations increased 300%
- **Target addressable market**: 150M+ Indians with mental health needs
